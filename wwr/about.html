Lhexic's Whitewater Rafting

Lhexic's Whitewater Rafting unleash the thrill of whitewater adventure, connecting people with nature and themselves. At Lhexic's Whitewater Rafting, we craft unforgettable river experiences, pushing limits and fostering courage in the rapids. Flow strong, ride wild, live free. "Dive into the rush" To be the leading whitewater destination, inspiring a global community of thrill-seekers and nature lovers.

History

Founded in 2020, Lhexic's Whitewater Rafting burst onto Ghana's adventure scene with a passion for whitewater thrills and a love for the Volta River. What started as a dream to explore Ghana's wildest rapids quickly grew into a community of river rebels, nature lovers, and thrill-seekers. Today, we're the go-to for epic rafting experiences, guiding adventurers through the untamed beauty of West Africa's best rivers
