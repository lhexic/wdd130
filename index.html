Hi My name is Jonathan Kwadjo Azanu and I from Ho, Ho is the capital city of the Ho Municipal District and the Volta Region of Ghana. Ho, the capital of Ghana's Volta Region, is a scenic, eco-friendly destination nestled between the Akwapim-Togo Ranges and Lake Volta. Known for the annual September Asogli Yam Festival, it offers tourists access to nearby attractions like the Wli Waterfalls, Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary, and Adaklu Mountain, alongside a vibrant local market culture
